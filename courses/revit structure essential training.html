REVIT STRUCTURE ESSENTIAL TRAINING

COURSE DETAILS

Learn how to design concrete buildings that stand the test of time with Revit Structure. We will take you step-by-step through the process, starting with laying out levels and a structural grid. Then we’ll show you how to add concrete columns and foundational elements like piers, pilasters, footings, and retaining walls, and add concrete framing and slabs. And of course, no concrete structure is complete without reinforcing: the final chapter guides you through the ins and outs of placing rebar perpendicular to a wall face, as well as area reinforcing and freely sketching rebar and selecting ties from the Rebar Shape Browser.

LEARNING OBJECTIVES

• Creating levels
• Creating a structural grid
• Adding single and multiple columns
• Laying foundations
• Creating perimeter framing and pan joists
• Placing concrete slabs
• Reinforcing with rebar
