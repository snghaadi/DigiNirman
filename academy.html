OUR MISSION

We live in a digital era where every sector is not only influenced but is also being revolutionized by technology. Like every other fied, education is also being re-shaped by digital technologies. Learners all over the world are prefering digital platforms to teach and train themselves. Meanwhile, language preferences and banking barrier is not allowing Nepalese learners to make use of digital learning platforms that are popular worldwide. Hence, Digicademy is an online learinng platform designed especially for people living in nepal who cannot access international e-learning platforms.
Digicademy is a platform developed by the company, Digi Nirman. Digi Nirman is working tirelessly to re shape construction industry through digital technologies. While the scope of bringing digital contents to share skills and knowledge are unlimited, digicademy is starting with courses related to engineering, project management and data analysis because of the expertise available through Digi Nirman in its initial phases.
Digicademy aims to grow in all possible catergories like, technology, marketing, business, language, entrance preparations etc. However, the courses available in Digicademy as of now is introduced in the following section.
